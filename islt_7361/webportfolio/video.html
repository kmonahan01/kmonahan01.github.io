Video Project

Project Requirements

  1. Video file must be between 30 - 60 seconds in length
  2. Video file must be no larger than 10 MB
  3. Video must fit on a webpage with screen resolution of 800 x 600 or less
  4. Video file must contain a separate audio track
  5. Video file must begin with a still title screen and end with credits that are easily readable
  6. Video file must contain one still image remaining on screen for at least 3 seconds that is in addition to the title and credits screens (not just a still frame, but an inserted image)
  7. Video file must contain at least 3 distinct motion video sequences
  8. Transitions between video sequences:
    • Must be a cut between sequences 1 and 2
    • Must be a cross-fade or dissolve between sequences 2 and 3

I chose to film one of my friends, her son, and them working with essential oils since I have recently gotten into them as well. They really work! Once my friend came over, I had my phone out and just casually filmed/took photos whenever I felt like I could potentially use something. Later that night, my husband helped me put the photos and videos onto his computer. From there I was able to click/drag/order the motion sequences and stills into the order I wanted. I was able to next record my own voiceover easily (thanks to Audacity!) To lower the audio in the motion sequences, I highlighted the sequence and clicked on the sound icon above the preview window, then selected the option to lower the volume. I added a title and credits page by clicking on the background options. and a dissolve/cross-fade to the last still by clicking on the transition options. Lastly, I adjusted the time by highlighting the portion I wanted and dragging left or right.
